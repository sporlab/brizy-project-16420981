SESSIONS

Unlock your football potential with our tailored 1-to-1 and small group coaching sessions for ages 4-16

Whether you're a beginner, looking to advance your skills, or aiming to reach elite levels, our 1-to-1 and small group coaching sessions are tailored just for you. We cater to ages 4-11 and 12-16, providing age-specific, personalized training at various venues in Hastings, Bexhill, Eastbourne, and surrounding areas.


Led by UEFA and The FA qualified coaches, our sessions focus on both technical and tactical development, ensuring a well-rounded growth in your football journey. Based on the FA's four corners model, we design sessions that align with your abilities, delivering the most effective training for your progress.


After your first session, we conduct an assessment to understand your strengths and areas for improvement, enabling us to create a customized coaching plan that suits your unique needs.


Take your football skills to the next level with our expert coaches and experience the joy of reaching your full potential on the field. Get in touch to book your private coaching session now!
