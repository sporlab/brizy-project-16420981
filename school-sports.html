We believe that physical education is an essential part of a child's development, and we are committed to promoting healthy lifestyles and positive attitudes towards sport and exercise.

Every child has the potential to succeed and thrive, and we work hard to create an environment that is supportive, inclusive, and fun.

At Skiltek, we are a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about providing high-quality physical education and activities to primary and secondary schools. Our range of activities includes PE classes, after-school clubs, and lunchtime activities. We also provide training for teachers to ensure they have the necessary skills to deliver fun and engaging sessions to their students.


We understand that every school has unique needs and requirements, which is why we tailor our offerings to meet the needs of each individual school. Our team of experienced coaches works closely with school staff to develop programs that suit the interests and abilities of their students.

How it Works?


At Skiltek, we are committed to providing a safe and enjoyable experience for all students. All our coaches are professionally qualified and have up-to-date DBS and safeguarding qualifications, as well as emergency first-aid training. We provide all equipment necessary for sessions and follow a structured approach to ensure consistent quality.


Our service is not limited to coaching. We will manage all bookings through our website. We also design a leaflet with your logo to promote the sessions at your school. We also offer ongoing support and guidance to ensure that our programs are successful and sustainable in the long term.


Join us at Skiltek for an exciting and safe experience for your students. Whether you are looking to enhance your existing physical education program or introduce new activities to your school, we have the expertise and resources to help you achieve your goals.
