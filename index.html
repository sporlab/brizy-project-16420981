SERVICES & PROJECTS

SCHOOL SPORTS

Physical education and exercise are essential part of a child's development, and we are committed to promoting healthy lifestyles and positive attitudes towards sport and exercise.

SOCCER SCHOOLS

Our soccer schools are typically run during half terms and summer holidays, and are aligned with the FA's Corner Module.

All sessions are delivered by experienced coaches and staff that regularly trained at highest standards.

COLLEGE PROGRAMMES

We oversee and deliver top-quality Academy and enrichment programs at several prestigious institutions, including Marsh Academy, Hastings College, Eastbourne College, and Lewes College.

DISABILITY & INCLUSIVE SESSIONS

As an accredited England Football disability recreation centre, we

deliver disability and inclusive sessions for individuals of all ages

who may have previously been excluded from participating in traditional sports.

GIRLS FOOTBALL

Inspired by the Lioness' Euro's success, our girls-only football program aims to encourage girls of all abilities and experience levels to get involved in football.

SPORT TOURS

We are committed to providing high-quality sports and cultural experiences that not only enhance physical fitness and technical skills, but also promote personal growth, cultural awareness, and social connections

USA SOCCER

Skiltek has the expertise, contacts, and know-how to help students who dream of studying and competing at the college level in the USA to achieve their goals.
